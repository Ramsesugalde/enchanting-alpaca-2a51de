Common Questions

Frequently Asked

No trucking experience required. CDL Alpha provides the complete curriculum, instructor certification standards, and operational playbook. You need business and management skills — we provide the industry expertise.

Requirements vary by state but generally include a business license, a state-approved truck driving school license, and compliance with FMCSA standards. Our onboarding team walks you through every step for your specific state — most franchisees complete this in 30–45 days.

The Starter tier works with 1 truck and can scale from there. Full Launch and Premium tiers typically start with 2–3 vehicles for optimal scheduling. You can lease vehicles to reduce upfront costs — our team will guide you through the options.

Most franchisees enroll their first students within 2–4 weeks of completing the onboarding process. CDL demand in most markets is extremely high — your marketing campaigns will often generate leads before you even open your doors.

We are currently expanding nationwide with priority markets in the Southeast, Midwest, Southwest, and Pacific Northwest. The best way to check availability in your target market is to submit an application — our team will confirm territory status within 48 hours.

CDL Alpha works with several SBA-friendly lenders and equipment financing partners who specialize in vocational training school businesses. We can connect you with these partners during your discovery call. Many franchisees finance a portion of their startup costs.
